Principles

One shall not be just PSD-to-HTML converter. Writing a fully correct is a some sort of Art.
Writing the code, you can be proud of - just a pleasure.

  • Fully responsive

    Mobile fist, and desktop best. All your customers must have a good experience.

  • A11y

Why me?

Most modern developers are ill: Tag Disorder, Div's Disease, Table Syndrome, Bootstrapis, Semanticless Infection. Many of them never get cured, or even never diagnose their illness. And result of their work, as a plague, spreads across the world.

My mission, and mission of couches of mine, is simple - Make web great again! And accessible to for everyone. And reusable and redoable by the next contractor or code owner.

I am not only doing things in time, with love and respect to all requirements. I am doing things right. Like any Senior WebDeveloper might do, but not usually doing by fact.

The right HTML code is the best for everyone - for a customer from PC, for a mobile customer, for disabled people, even for search bot. And the right HTML code - is a cheap code. As long it should be written just once, and might have close to zero maintenance cost.
